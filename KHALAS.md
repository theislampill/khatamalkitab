# KHALAS

## Purpose
`KHALAS.md` is the repository's cumulative synthesis page. Its job is to gather the strongest verse pages, passage pages, clusters, and convergences into one disciplined but affirmative judgment about the biblical prophetic expectation. It does not reproduce working-corpus language. It presents the repo's ranked argument as a coherent whole.

## Source-Status Legend
- `[anchored]`: directly tied to promoted passage or cluster pages
- `[synthesis]`: produced by joining multiple promoted pages without inventing a new source base
- `[inference]`: a higher-order conclusion warranted by the mapped structure
- `[speculative]`: too thin or too unstable to govern the conclusion
- `[control]`: used to discipline the argument rather than to serve as direct evidence

## Argument Order
The repository is strongest when read in a ranked sequence rather than as a flat pile of proof texts:

1. the direct profile supplied by [Prophet Like Moses](arguments/prophet-like-moses/summary.md) and [Paraclete](arguments/paraclete/summary.md)
2. the reinforcement supplied by [Primary Profile Convergence](convergences/primary-profile-convergence/summary.md)
3. the field-narrowing work supplied by [Arabian Servant And Kedar](arguments/arabian-servant-and-kedar/summary.md), [Ishmaelite Line And Brethren](arguments/ishmaelite-line-and-brethren/summary.md), and [Paran And Arabia](arguments/paran-and-arabia/summary.md)
4. the reinforcement supplied by [Candidate-Field Narrowing](convergences/candidate-field-narrowing/summary.md)
5. the anti-closure and discipline layer supplied by [Live Prophetic Expectation](arguments/live-prophetic-expectation/summary.md), [Messenger Before Arrival](arguments/messenger-before-arrival/summary.md), and [Law Continuity Control](arguments/law-continuity-control/summary.md)
6. the broader public-horizon layer supplied by [Public Guidance Convergence](convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md)
7. the corroborative lines, which add fit without governing the thesis

That order matters because the repo does not ask weaker material to do the work of stronger material, and it does not confuse dependent bridges or controls with fresh direct evidence.

## Primary Spine
[anchored] The cumulative case begins with the two primary clusters because they define the kind of figure the thesis must find before any historical narrowing can become persuasive. [Prophet Like Moses](arguments/prophet-like-moses/summary.md) gives the Torah-side office profile: a figure raised by God, like Moses, entrusted with received speech, and joined to serious accountability for response. [Paraclete](arguments/paraclete/summary.md) gives the Gospel-side later-guide profile: a post-Jesus figure marked by witness, guidance, future disclosure, and speech received rather than self-originated.

[synthesis] Those two lines become materially stronger when read through [Primary Profile Convergence](convergences/primary-profile-convergence/summary.md). The convergence is important not because it manufactures a third proof, but because it shows that the direct profile is not coming from one canonical location alone. One line supplies office, mediation, and accountable speech. The other supplies later timing, witness to Jesus, and forward disclosure. That is a genuine increase of force, not redundancy.

[synthesis] The dependent bridge in [Prophet Like Moses And Paraclete](arguments/prophet-like-moses-and-paraclete/summary.md) helps clarify why received speech is a structural feature of the repo's direct-profile architecture. It remains explicitly dependent so the evidence count does not get inflated.

## Candidate-Field Narrowing
[synthesis] Once the direct profile remains live, the next question is where the surviving candidate field most plausibly points. That is where the repo's three-part narrowing complex becomes indispensable. [Arabian Servant And Kedar](arguments/arabian-servant-and-kedar/summary.md) contributes a public mission profile joined to Kedar-facing geography. [Ishmaelite Line And Brethren](arguments/ishmaelite-line-and-brethren/summary.md) contributes candidate eligibility and kinship pressure. [Paran And Arabia](arguments/paran-and-arabia/summary.md) contributes repeated regional direction.

[synthesis] These clusters are strongest when gathered in [Candidate-Field Narrowing](convergences/candidate-field-narrowing/summary.md). That convergence matters because each member adds a different constraint. One adds mission shape. One adds eligibility. One adds geographic direction. Read together, they do not merely say "Arabia" several times. They narrow the field by non-identical means. This is one of the most important architectural gains in the repository, because it explains why the cumulative case becomes historically directional rather than remaining a set of interesting but disconnected motifs.

## Anti-Closure And Discipline
[synthesis] The cumulative argument also needs an anti-closure layer. Without it, the reader may feel obliged to treat the field as already settled before the stronger direct and narrowing clusters are even considered. [Live Prophetic Expectation](arguments/live-prophetic-expectation/summary.md) keeps expectation categories visibly alive. [Messenger Before Arrival](arguments/messenger-before-arrival/summary.md) adds messenger-sequence pressure in a contested but still useful way. [Law Continuity Control](arguments/law-continuity-control/summary.md) keeps later fulfillment language from becoming careless replacement rhetoric.

[synthesis] Gathered in [Anti-Closure Convergence](convergences/anti-closure-convergence/summary.md), these pages do not identify the Prophet (peace be upon him) directly. They do something different and still necessary: they make room for the stronger argument to be heard. They show that the field remains interpretively alive, that messenger and restoration themes can include sequence rather than instant closure, and that serious fulfillment claims must remain covenant-aware. That layer helps the whole repo sound more coherent and more persuasive because it removes premature closure without pretending that context pages are direct proofs.

## Public Scope And Historical Horizon
[synthesis] The repo does not stop at a private profile. [Public Guidance Convergence](convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md) shows how later guidance, servant law-to-nations pressure, and the broader culmination motifs can reinforce one another. This line remains lower in rank than the primary profile convergence and the candidate-field narrowing convergence, but it still matters. It helps explain why the cumulative case points toward a public guidance horizon rather than only toward a private or inward fulfillment.

[inference] [Kingdom Transfer](arguments/kingdom-transfer/summary.md) belongs here rather than at the front of the case. Its broad historical and culmination motifs do not establish the thesis directly, but they can widen the horizon of plausibility once the stronger lines are already standing.

## Corroborative Fit
[inference] The repo retains several corroborative lines because cumulative cases often become more intelligible at the edges once their center is already secure. [Unlettered Prophet](arguments/unlettered-prophet/summary.md), [Baca And Pilgrimage](arguments/baca-and-pilgrimage/summary.md), and [Mahmad And Praised-One](arguments/mahmad-and-praised-one/summary.md) remain in the project at that rank. Their legitimate role is secondary fit, not governance. They can deepen the cumulative impression; they cannot replace the primary spine or the main narrowing complex.

This rank distinction matters. If one of these corroborative lines weakens, the cumulative case does not collapse. Their value is asymmetrical: they enrich the direction already established by stronger convergences without supplying the direction by themselves.

## Main Rival Pressure
[control] The strongest rival pressures remain concentrated in the primary clusters and their major controls. Deuteronomy 18 still faces succession and Jesus-centered closure readings. The Paraclete cluster still faces Spirit and Pentecost closure readings. The servant and geography material still faces serious local and canonical alternatives. The anti-closure layer remains contextual rather than identificatory. None of that is hidden here.

[inference] Even so, the cumulative argument no longer stands or falls on isolated verses, on lexical surprise, or on one place-name argument. It stands or falls on whether the two primary profiles remain genuinely live, whether the three-part narrowing complex adds real historical direction, and whether the combined structure still points more coherently in one direction than in its rivals. That is a far more substantial claim than a checklist of proof texts, and it is exactly the kind of claim the repo is now built to present.

## Final Judgment
[inference] On the architecture presently mapped in the repository, the strongest cumulative route remains affirmative. A Moses-like bearer of received divine speech and accountable authority, together with a post-Jesus guide or witness whose speech is not self-originated and whose role is bound to truth and future disclosure, forms the direct profile spine of the case. When that surviving direct profile is then narrowed by servant mission, lineage eligibility, and Arabian geography, the argument becomes historically directional rather than merely suggestive. When expectation, messenger-sequence, and continuity controls are added in their proper subordinate place, the case becomes not only stronger but more coherent.

[inference] The repository's conclusion is therefore not that every promoted passage independently names the Prophet (peace be upon him), nor that all rival readings are trivial. It is that, once the pages are ranked correctly and read through their actual argumentative roles, the strongest surviving lines converge most coherently on the Prophet (peace be upon him). That is why the repo's thesis is cumulative, serious, and thesis-serving rather than fragile. The argument becomes forceful not by hiding its controls, but by placing them correctly beneath a constructive and convergent case that remains stronger than the alternatives it has already taken into account.
