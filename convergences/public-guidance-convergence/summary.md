# Public Guidance Convergence

`Status:` [synthesis]. `Rank:` medium convergence.

## Convergence Claim
This convergence joins the later-guide profile with the servant law-to-nations profile and the repo's broader culmination material. Its purpose is to show that the case does not terminate in a merely private prophet claim. It develops toward a public guidance horizon marked by witness, law-bearing mission, and broader historical scope.

## What Each Side Adds
[Paraclete](../../arguments/paraclete/summary.md) contributes later guidance, witness, and disclosure. [Arabian Servant And Kedar](../../arguments/arabian-servant-and-kedar/summary.md) contributes public mission, justice, and law-to-nations pressure. [Kingdom Transfer](../../arguments/kingdom-transfer/summary.md) contributes broad culmination and transfer motifs at a lower but still meaningful rank. The convergence matters because it helps the repo explain why the cumulative case points beyond a hidden or purely inward fulfillment.

## Why This Is Not Redundancy
Guidance, law-bearing mission, and historical culmination are related but not identical ideas. The first concerns the later figure's role. The second concerns the public scope of that role. The third concerns the broader historical horizon into which such a role might fit. That distinction is what makes the convergence additive rather than repetitive.

## Objection Note
This convergence remains lower in rank than the primary profile convergence and the candidate-field narrowing convergence because its historical breadth is more synthetic and its kingdom material is more debated. Even so, it contributes a valuable explanatory layer once the stronger lines are already in place.

## Contribution Summary
This convergence strengthens the thesis by giving the cumulative case public scale. It shows how later guidance, wider mission, and historical culmination can reinforce one another without being mistaken for the primary foundation of the argument.
