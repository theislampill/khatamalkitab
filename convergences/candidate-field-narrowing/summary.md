# Candidate-Field Narrowing

`Status:` [synthesis]. `Rank:` high-value convergence.

## Convergence Claim

This convergence is where the case becomes historically directional. Once a surviving direct profile is in view, the argument has to ask where such a figure could plausibly belong. Three different lines answer that question from different angles. The servant material supplies mission shape and public-law force. The lineage material keeps a particular candidate field textually eligible. The Arabian material gives directional pressure through settlement, regional recurrence, and emergence sequence. Together they do not merely decorate the case. They narrow it.

## How Each Line Constrains The Search

[Arabian Servant And Kedar](../../arguments/arabian-servant-and-kedar/summary.md) contributes more than geographic color. It shows that justice, public mission, law-bearing force, widened praise, Kedar-facing language, and even sanctuary-linked Arabian overlap can belong to one servant sequence. That matters because it gives the case a mission-shaped criterion instead of leaving the field undefined. [Ishmaelite Line And Brethren](../../arguments/ishmaelite-line-and-brethren/summary.md) contributes a different kind of force. It does not identify the candidate; it keeps an Ishmaelite and brethren-related field from being excluded before the case is even heard. [Paran And Arabia](../../arguments/paran-and-arabia/summary.md) adds the third constraint by turning repeated Arabian and Paran-related material into directional field pressure and emergence sequence rather than a single-point proof.

The combination matters because each line answers a question the others cannot answer alone. Mission profile asks what sort of public role is in view. Lineage asks which field remains eligible. Emergence sequence asks where the surviving field begins to gather and point. The cumulative result is stronger than any one of the three because the lines are not redundant. They constrain the search by different means.

## Why The Combination Matters More Than The Parts

If the servant material stood alone, the case would still face the objection that public mission and law-to-nations themes are too broad to identify any one field. If lineage stood alone, the case would still be open to the objection that genealogy proves nothing beyond textual availability. If Arabian material stood alone, the case would still look like place-name accumulation. The convergence matters because it prevents each line from remaining isolated in its own weakness. Mission gives direction to lineage. Lineage keeps geography from becoming arbitrary. Emergence sequence keeps mission and eligibility from remaining abstract.

That is why this page does more than say that three secondary clusters all point southward in some loose sense. It shows how the case moves from a surviving prophet-profile to a plausible candidate field without leaping over its middle steps. Once that movement is in place, the cumulative argument no longer looks like a scattered set of motifs. It looks like a narrowing route.

## Boundary And Discipline

This convergence still has limits, and naming them here strengthens rather than weakens it. Geography can be overstated. Lineage can be mistaken for proof. Servant and Kedar language can be detached from their local alternatives. But those limits are exactly why the convergence must be read as a convergence. The point is not that one member settles the thesis. The point is that, after the direct profile survives, these different secondary lines make the same field look more plausible for different reasons.

## Contribution Summary

This convergence strengthens the thesis by giving the repo a disciplined route from direct profile to plausible historical field. It is the chief reason the cumulative argument looks directional rather than abstract.
