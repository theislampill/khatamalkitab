`Status:` [synthesis]. `Rank:` medium-high convergence.

## Convergence Claim
This convergence brings together the expectation, messenger-sequence, and continuity-control layers. Its job is not to provide direct proof. Its job is to show why the reader is not required to treat the field as already closed before the stronger direct and narrowing clusters are even considered.

## What Each Side Adds
[Live Prophetic Expectation](../../arguments/live-prophetic-expectation/summary.md) shows that expectation categories remained active and differentiated. [Messenger Before Arrival](../../arguments/messenger-before-arrival/summary.md) adds sequence and preparation pressure. [Law Continuity Control](../../arguments/law-continuity-control/summary.md) adds a discipline against crude replacement rhetoric. The result is a convergence that keeps the cumulative case open, serious, and methodologically proportionate.

## Why This Is Not Redundancy
Expectation, messenger sequence, and continuity do not do the same work. One says the field is still alive. One says the field may include preparatory stages. One says later fulfillment language must remain covenant-aware. Because their functions differ, they reinforce one another without simply restating the same claim.

## Objection Note
The most obvious objection is that these lines are contextual rather than identificatory. That is correct, and it is already built into the rank of the page. Their value lies precisely in being contextual. They do not identify the Prophet (peace be upon him) directly, but they remove several premature closure moves that would otherwise prevent the stronger parts of the case from being heard.

This convergence strengthens the thesis by protecting the argumentative field in which the primary and secondary clusters operate. It keeps the repo's case from being shut down too quickly and keeps the resulting cumulative argument more credible.
