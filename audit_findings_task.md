# Audit Findings Task

This document is the governing audit pack for the current top-level alignment phase. It is source-neutral and does not expose private corpus identity or wording. Its job is to separate genuinely strong lanes from pseudo-broad repeated hubs, identify where the current public repo still under-realizes the case, and provide a concrete execution order for the next meaningful rewrite task.

## 1. Executive Findings

- The earlier source-extraction work was directionally right at the top level but materially incomplete in the middle layer. The main miss was not the discovery of the primary lanes; it was the under-description of how the corpus moves from direct profile to differentiated expectation, from expectation to narrowing, and from narrowing to public guidance and cumulative judgment.
- The private corpus is genuinely strong in five areas: the Mosaic office profile, the Johannine later-guide profile, the servant public-law profile, the Ishmaelite or brethren eligibility field, and the Arabian emergence field. It is also strong in differentiated expectation and law-continuity control, but those serve support and discipline roles rather than direct identification.
- The corpus is pseudo-broad in several places. Lexical naming, unlettered or recitation material, Baca or sacred geography, and broad historical-transfer material recur across many corpus items, but much of that recurrence is repeated reuse of a small set of motifs rather than genuinely diverse inferential support.
- The public repo is no longer dominated by explicit objection blocks, which is a real improvement. The remaining problem is now argumentative density. Too many pages still rely on short claim shells, stock connective sentences, and architecture-summary language instead of unfolding the inference itself.
- The support layers beneath `KHALAS.md` have now been materially strengthened, and `KHALAS.md` itself has been rebuilt as an integrative cumulative thesis page. The main remaining top-level task is no longer architectural rescue but production alignment: keeping the public face of the repo coherent, disciplined, and visibly faithful to the route it now presents.
