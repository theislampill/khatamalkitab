# Deuteronomy 18:18-19

## Claim Use
This local range is used to argue not only for a future prophet like Moses, but also for the obligation to hear that prophet once he appears. It is the main Deuteronomy cluster in the working corpus.

## Why This Range Matters Together
- Verse 18 gives the prophet's profile.
- Verse 19 adds the warning that refusal has consequences.
- Many working arguments treat the range as a single commissioning statement rather than as two separable proof texts.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Many readers understand the passage as part of the broader Israelite prophetic office.
- Some Christian readings link the range to Jesus directly, especially when Acts 3 is read as a definitive interpretation.
