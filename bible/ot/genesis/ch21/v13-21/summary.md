# Genesis 21:13-21

## Claim Use
This local range is used for the Ishmael-Paran line: Ishmael is preserved, made into a nation, and associated with the wilderness of Paran.

## Why This Range Matters Together
- The promise, exile, rescue, and settlement belong to one narrative block.
- "Paran" is central to later geography-based argument clusters.
- The range is used as background support for Deuteronomy 33 and Arabian-location arguments.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- The passage is narrative before it is prophetic.
- Later arguments depend on how Paran is identified and whether the narrative can bear later typological use.
