# Zechariah 9:9

`Status:` anchored.

## Claim
This verse contributes the arrival of a righteous and humble king. It matters because the widening line does not begin with imperial display but with a form of rule marked by justice and lowliness.

## Reasoning
On its own, the verse is not yet the full public-order horizon. It establishes the character of the ruler whose arrival matters. That is why it belongs in the widening family: the quality of the figure's rule prepares for the broader peace-and-dominion movement that follows.

## Networked Significance
This supports [Kingdom Transfer](../../../../../arguments/kingdom-transfer/summary.md) and, more lightly, [Public Guidance Convergence](../../../../../convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md) by showing that widened rule is supposed to be righteous and peaceable in kind.
