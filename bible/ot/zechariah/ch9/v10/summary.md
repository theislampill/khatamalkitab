# Zechariah 9:10

`Status:` anchored.

## Claim
This verse is where the widening turn becomes explicit: war instruments are cut off, peace is spoken to the nations, and dominion extends broadly. It matters because the public-order layer is stated here in concrete terms rather than in vague historical mood.

## Reasoning
The verse links rule to disarmament, peace, and widened reach. That is why it functions as a useful secondary support page: it gives the widening family a genuinely public horizon without pretending to be the main proof by itself.

## Networked Significance
This strengthens [Kingdom Transfer](../../../../../arguments/kingdom-transfer/summary.md) and also helps [Public Guidance Convergence](../../../../../convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md) by making the widening line speak about peaceable public consequence rather than bare expansion.
