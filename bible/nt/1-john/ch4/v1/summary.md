# 1 John 4:1

`Status:` anchored.

## Claim
John opens this passage by forbidding automatic trust in spiritual claims. That matters because the Johannine material is not framed as permission to accept anything said in the name of the Spirit.

## Reasoning
The verse establishes the need for testing before it gives the detailed test. False prophetic claims are already active, so discernment is not optional. This makes the verse a genuine control on careless spiritual enthusiasm.
