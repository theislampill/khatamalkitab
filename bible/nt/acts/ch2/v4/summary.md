# Acts 2:4

`Status:` anchored. `Role:` Paraclete control anchor / Spirit-speaking event. `Contribution:` control; high as a Pentecost fulfillment test.

## Claim
This verse contributes the key Pentecost claim: the disciples are filled with the Holy Spirit and speak as the Spirit gives utterance. It matters because it is the sharpest event-level control on any claim that the Paraclete promise was not realized in early Christian Spirit empowerment.
