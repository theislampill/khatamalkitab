# Acts 2:4

## Page Type
Atomic passage page

## Passage-Specific Contribution
- This verse contributes the key Pentecost claim: the disciples are filled with the Holy Spirit and speak as the Spirit gives utterance.
- It matters because it is the sharpest event-level control on any claim that the Paraclete promise was not realized in early Christian Spirit empowerment.
- The strongest alternative is that Pentecost may still be a real Spirit event without exhaustively satisfying every feature of John 16.
