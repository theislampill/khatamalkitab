# Acts 21:25

`Status:` anchored.

## Claim
This verse records the separate apostolic decision regarding Gentile believers. It matters because the scene does not present one flat rule for everyone; it shows differentiated judgment inside the continuity question.

## Reasoning
That distinction is exactly what makes the verse useful as a control. It keeps the larger case from collapsing into either total sameness or total rupture. The narrative recognizes differentiated communal obligations while still treating continuity as a serious matter.
