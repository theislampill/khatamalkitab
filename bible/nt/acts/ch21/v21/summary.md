`Status:` anchored.

## Claim
This verse names the exact rumor at issue: Paul is accused of teaching Jews among the nations to forsake Moses. It matters because the control becomes concrete at the point of alleged discontinuity.

## Reasoning
The accusation is sharper than a generic debate about law. It concerns teaching, practice, and covenantal identity. That specificity is what gives the verse control value and keeps later cumulative arguments from flattening continuity into an already-settled matter.
