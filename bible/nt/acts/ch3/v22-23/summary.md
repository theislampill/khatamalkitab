# Acts 3:22-23

## Claim Use
This range records the New Testament reuse of Deuteronomy 18. In the repository it matters because it shows how the prophet-like-Moses text is explicitly received inside early Christian preaching.

## Why This Range Matters Together
- Verse 22 cites the Mosaic expectation.
- Verse 23 adds the obligation and warning.
- The range is the cleanest NT bridge back to Deuteronomy 18.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- For many readers, this range settles the matter in favor of Jesus.
- In this repo it functions less as a proof for Muhammad than as a required objection-and-reception node.
