# John 16:9

## Page Type
Atomic passage page

## Passage-Specific Contribution
- This verse specifies the first element of the conviction triad: the world is convicted concerning sin because it does not believe in Jesus.
- It matters because the Paraclete sequence is not just inward comfort; it publicly exposes unbelief in relation to Jesus.
- The strongest alternative is that Jesus is describing the Holy Spirit's post-resurrection/Pentecost work.
