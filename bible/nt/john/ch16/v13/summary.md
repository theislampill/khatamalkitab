# John 16:13

`Status:` anchored. `Role:` major atomic anchor inside the Paraclete range. `Contribution:` high-value local anchor with strong comparison pressure.

## Claim
This verse concentrates the later-guide profile into one reusable statement: guidance into truth, speech that is received rather than self-originated, and disclosure of what is to come. That makes it the most important atomic node inside the Paraclete line for later speech-profile and convergence work.

## Reasoning
The verse matters because it gathers several high-value predicates into one place. It does not merely say that guidance will continue. It specifies how that guidance is characterized: it is truth-bearing, future-facing, and non-autonomous in speech. At atom level, that gives the repository a compact statement of the later-guide profile without requiring the verse to settle the larger identity question by itself.

## Local Significance
Within its local setting, John 16:13 preserves the densest verse-level form of the Paraclete's received-speech logic. That is why the repo reuses it so often at higher levels. It carries more local argumentative density than many surrounding verses, even though the full range remains necessary for proper context.

## Networked Significance
Within the wider repository, this verse strengthens [Paraclete](../../../../../arguments/paraclete/summary.md), [Paraclete And Subordination](../../../../../arguments/paraclete-and-subordination/summary.md), [Prophet Like Moses And Paraclete](../../../../../arguments/prophet-like-moses-and-paraclete/summary.md), and [Primary Profile Convergence](../../../../../convergences/primary-profile-convergence/summary.md). It is one of the atomic passages that allows the Gospel-side argument to move from local reading into profile convergence.

## Objection Note
The main competing reading is that Jesus is describing the Holy Spirit's post-resurrection and Pentecost guidance of the disciples and early community. That objection sets the verse's real interpretive boundary. Even so, the verse still contributes a major local predicate, because the debate is over whether that predicate is fully exhausted by the standard closure reading, not over whether the verse has any direct force.

## Contribution Summary
Used this way, John 16:13 strengthens the thesis by supplying one of the repo's strongest atomic statements of the Gospel-side later-guide profile.
