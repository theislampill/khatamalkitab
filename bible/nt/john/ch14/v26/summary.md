# John 14:26

## Claim Use
This verse is the most contested atomic Paraclete text in the working corpus because it is the only major Johannine line here that explicitly associates the Paraclete with the Holy Spirit.

## Why This Passage Gets Its Own Node
- Text-critical and manuscript arguments concentrate on this verse.
- It is often treated as the main objection to a human-prophet reading of the Paraclete.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Christian readings usually treat this verse as decisive.
- Several working files argue that the wording is textually unstable or interpretively narrower than John 16, but those claims require separate verification.
