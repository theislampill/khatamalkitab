# John 14:16-17

## Claim Use
This local range is the opening Paraclete block: another advocate, abiding presence, and the title "Spirit of truth." It is a basic entry point for the Paraclete cluster.

## Why This Range Matters Together
- The identity claim depends on both "another" and "Spirit of truth."
- The working corpus regularly uses the range, not just verse 16, when arguing for a future human messenger.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Mainstream Christian readings identify the figure as the Holy Spirit.
- The wording about abiding and indwelling is heavily debated and is not resolved by these verses alone.
