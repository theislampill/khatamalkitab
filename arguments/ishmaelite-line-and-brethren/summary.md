# Ishmaelite Line and Brethren

## Cluster Claim
This cluster assembles the supporting texts used to argue that the "brethren" language in Deuteronomy can point beyond Israel to the Ishmael-related line.

## Depends On
- `bible/ot/genesis/ch16/v12/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/genesis/ch17/v20/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/genesis/ch21/v13-21/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/deuteronomy/ch18/v18/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/ezekiel/ch27/v21/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/isaiah/ch42/v11/summary.md`

## Main Use
- Keep identity and lineage support separate from direct prophecy claims.
- Route Ishmael, Kedar, and Paran material into a reusable background cluster.
- Show where geography and genealogy are doing argumentative work.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- None of these passages alone names a later prophet.
- The cluster is strongest as background support and weakest when treated as a standalone proof.
