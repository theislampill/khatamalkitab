# Kingdom Transfer

## Cluster Claim
This cluster gathers passages used to argue that authority, rule, fruit-bearing, or covenantal leadership shifts away from earlier holders toward a later nation or kingdom.

## Main Use
- Group Shiloh, kingdom, stone, and transfer-of-rule arguments together.
- Separate political or kingdom-transfer reasoning from the more direct prophet-like-Moses and Paraclete clusters.
- Provide a single place for reviewing whether these readings are genuinely prophetic, typological, or overextended.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- These readings often require the largest historical and political synthesis steps in the repository.
- Many of the individual passages can be read in more local historical frames, so cumulative reasoning is essential here.
