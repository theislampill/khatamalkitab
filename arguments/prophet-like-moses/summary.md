# Prophet Like Moses

## Cluster Claim
This argument cluster treats Deuteronomy 18 as the primary prophecy and uses John 1 and Acts 3 to track how that expectation is carried into the New Testament.

## Depends On
- `bible/ot/deuteronomy/ch18/v18/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/deuteronomy/ch18/v18-19/summary.md`
- `bible/nt/john/ch1/v19-21/summary.md`
- `bible/nt/acts/ch3/v22-23/summary.md`

## Main Use
- Establish a future prophet-profile centered on likeness to Moses.
- Track whether the expected figure is distinguishable from the Messiah in John 1.
- Record Acts 3 as the major early-Christian reception of the passage.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Christian interpretation often treats Acts 3 as settling the question in favor of Jesus.
- Muslim apologetic readings typically argue that Deuteronomy 18 still deserves narrower comparison on lawgiving, polity, and revealed speech.
