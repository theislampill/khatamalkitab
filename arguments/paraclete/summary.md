# Paraclete

`Status:` [anchored] + [synthesis]. `Rank:` primary, load-bearing direct cluster.

## Cluster Claim
This cluster supplies the repository's strongest Gospel-side later-guide profile. John 14-16 does not merely mention a helper. It presents a figure whose coming is bound to Jesus' departure, whose work includes witness, guidance, public exposure or conviction, future disclosure, and speech received rather than self-originated. Taken together, those features create the second major direct profile in the repo.

## Why This Cluster Matters
The cluster's passages form one argumentative sequence. [John 14:16-17](../../bible/nt/john/ch14/v16-17/summary.md) introduces another guide and the truth-bearing frame. [John 14:26](../../bible/nt/john/ch14/v26/summary.md) adds teaching and remembrance. [John 15:26](../../bible/nt/john/ch15/v26/summary.md) adds witness to Jesus. [John 16:7-13](../../bible/nt/john/ch16/v7-13/summary.md) adds departure-conditioned coming, public conviction, deferred disclosure, future disclosure, and non-autonomous speech. [John 12:49](../../bible/nt/john/ch12/v49/summary.md) matters as an internal speech-profile control because it shows that received speech is already part of Johannine revelation language. The result is a profile cluster, not a single lexical argument.

## Distinctive Force
This cluster adds things the Torah-side primary cluster cannot add by itself. It contributes explicit post-Jesus timing, witness-to-Jesus logic, public world-facing action, and future disclosure. That is why the cluster is not a duplicate of Deuteronomy 18. It is a second direct line, from a different part of the canon, that asks whether a later truth-bearing and witness-bearing figure remains in view after Jesus' departure.

## Networked Significance
Within the architecture, this cluster anchors [Primary Profile Convergence](../../convergences/primary-profile-convergence/summary.md) by supplying the Gospel-side half of the direct profile. It also feeds [Public Guidance Convergence](../../convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md), where the later-guide profile is joined to broader law-to-nations and mission-pressure material. The cluster is further clarified by [Paraclete And Subordination](../paraclete-and-subordination/summary.md), which keeps Johannine dependence and speech categories visible without turning those control passages into a separate proof engine.

## Objection Note
The dominant rival synthesis is substantial: the Paraclete is commonly identified with the Holy Spirit, the post-resurrection gift, Pentecost, and the life of the early community. That rival reading belongs at cluster level because it governs the whole family of passages. The repository therefore treats it as the main boundary on the cluster. Even so, the cluster continues to matter constructively because the case does not rise or fall on wordplay. It rises or falls on whether the whole later-guide profile is fully exhausted by that internal closure reading.

## Contribution Summary
This cluster strengthens the thesis by securing the second primary pillar of the cumulative case: a post-Jesus guide or witness whose speech is received, whose mission remains tied to Jesus, and whose role is forward-looking rather than merely retrospective. Together with the Mosaic office profile, it makes the cumulative argument more than a single proof-text exercise.
