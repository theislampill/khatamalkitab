# Paraclete And Subordination

`Status:` [control] + [synthesis]. `Rank:` dependent control cluster.

## Control Claim
This cluster keeps Johannine dependence and speech categories visible while the Paraclete material is being interpreted. Its role is not to supply a separate prophecy proof. Its role is to make sure the Gospel-side argument is read through John's own language of received speech, dependence, mission, and divine authorization rather than through later shorthand that erases those categories.

## Why This Control Matters
[John 12:49](../../bible/nt/john/ch12/v49/summary.md) and [John 16:13](../../bible/nt/john/ch16/v13/summary.md) foreground received speech. [John 14:28](../../bible/nt/john/ch14/v28/summary.md), [John 17:3](../../bible/nt/john/ch17/v3/summary.md), [John 20:17](../../bible/nt/john/ch20/v17/summary.md), and [Mark 13:32](../../bible/nt/mark/ch13/v32/summary.md) preserve dependence and mission language. [John 1:1](../../bible/nt/john/ch1/v1/summary.md) and [John 17:5](../../bible/nt/john/ch17/v5/summary.md) matter here because they supply the strongest counter-pressure from high Christology and preexistence readings. That is why the cluster is valuable as a control family rather than as a free-standing argument page.

## Constructive Use
This control cluster helps the repo in two ways. First, it prevents a shallow dismissal of the Paraclete material by making Johannine agency and speech categories harder to ignore. Second, it prevents overreach by reminding the reader that these passages are interpretive controls, not direct prophecy claims. Used that way, the cluster sharpens the Gospel-side case without distorting its rank.

## Networked Significance
This page remains dependent on [Paraclete](../paraclete/summary.md) and supports [Primary Profile Convergence](../../convergences/primary-profile-convergence/summary.md) indirectly. It also helps [Public Guidance Convergence](../../convergences/public-guidance-convergence/summary.md) by keeping the Gospel-side guidance language tied to public witness and received speech rather than vague spirituality.

## Objection Note
The chief rival synthesis integrates these passages into orthodox Christology and Trinitarian mission. That is why the cluster should not be treated as a separate proof. Its contribution lies in interpretive pressure and clarification. It strengthens the repo when it remains a control cluster and weakens it when it tries to become more.

## Contribution Summary
This cluster strengthens the thesis indirectly but importantly. It helps the Paraclete material remain textually anchored, keeps Johannine categories in play, and improves the discipline of the whole Gospel-side argument.
