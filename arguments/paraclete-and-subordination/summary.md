# Paraclete and Subordination

## Cluster Claim
This cluster tracks the recurring corpus move that reads the Paraclete sayings alongside Johannine subordination passages rather than in isolation.

## Depends On
- `bible/nt/john/ch14/v28/summary.md`
- `bible/nt/john/ch14/v13-17/summary.md`
- `bible/nt/john/ch14/v26/summary.md`

- `bible/nt/john/ch16/v14-15/summary.md`
- `bible/nt/john/ch12/v49/summary.md`

## Main Use
- Keep the composite John 14-16 plus subordination argument out of a single verse page.
- Show how "the Father is greater than I" and "he will not speak on his own" are used together in the corpus.
- Provide a review point for whether the Johannine material is being read as prophetic succession, non-Trinitarian control material, or both.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- These readings depend on broader theological judgments about John's Gospel.
- The cluster should not be confused with the narrower question of whether a single Paraclete verse names Muhammad directly.
