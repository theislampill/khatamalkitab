# Baca and Pilgrimage

## Cluster Claim
This cluster gathers the Baca / springs / pilgrimage material used to connect Psalm 84 and related location arguments to Makkah-oriented readings.

## Depends On
- `bible/ot/psalms/ch84/v4-10/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/song-of-songs/ch6/v2/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/isaiah/ch60/v1-12/summary.md`

## Main Use
- Keep Baca material out of unrelated prophet-like-Moses pages.
- Separate pilgrimage and location arguments from the main Paraclete and Deuteronomy clusters.
- Provide a single home for Baca, springs, and house-of-God style arguments.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Baca may be read as figurative or non-Makkah geography.
- The move from pilgrimage imagery to Makkah remains interpretive and should be argued cumulatively, not by one line alone.
