# Paran And Arabia

## Central Thesis
[synthesis] This cluster narrows the geography field. Ishmael/Paran, theophany language, Arabia/Tema/Dedan/Kedar, and Isaiah 42's Kedar/Sela material converge as support for an Arabian candidate field, not as direct proof.

## Why These Passages Belong Together
[Genesis 21:13-21](../../bible/ot/genesis/ch21/v13-21/summary.md) places Ishmael's survival and settlement in the wilderness/Paran frame. [Deuteronomy 33:1-2](../../bible/ot/deuteronomy/ch33/v1-2/summary.md) and [Habakkuk 3:3](../../bible/ot/habakkuk/ch3/v3/summary.md) contribute Paran/Teman theophany language. [Isaiah 21:13-17](../../bible/ot/isaiah/ch21/v13-17/summary.md) supplies Arabia, Tema, Dedan, and Kedar pressure in a local Arabian scene. [Ezekiel 27:21](../../bible/ot/ezekiel/ch27/v21/summary.md) and [Isaiah 42:11](../../bible/ot/isaiah/ch42/v11/summary.md) remain bridge texts that keep Kedar connected to the wider Arabian map, but they are not the anchor core of this cluster.

## Cumulative Profile
Each passage adds a different constraint: lineage-location, revelatory geography, Arabian names, Kedar linkage, and servant-cluster contact. Together they help answer “where does the candidate field point?” They do not answer “who is the prophet?” without the direct clusters.

## Strongest Alternative Synthesis
Deuteronomy 33 and Habakkuk 3 may be divine-warrior poetry recalling YHWH's coming from Sinai/Edom/Teman/Paran. Isaiah 21 may describe local ancient Arabian events. If those local readings govern fully, the cluster remains geography resonance only.

## Inference Boundary
[synthesis] The relevant place-name field is constructed from anchored passages and supporting bridge texts. Its relevance depends on primary prophecy clusters and Isaiah 42. [inference] Arabian fulfillment is a cumulative judgment, not a single-place-name conclusion.

## Load-Bearing Status
Secondary candidate-field support. It is semi-load-bearing for geography, not direct prophecy.

## Failure Modes
- Do not make one place-name carry the whole case.
- Do not suppress the theophany reading.
- Do not detach geography from the primary prophet profile.
