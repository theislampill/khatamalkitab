# Paran and Arabia

## Cluster Claim
This cluster gathers passages used to argue that a later revelatory center emerges from Paran / Arabia rather than from Israel alone.

## Depends On
- `bible/ot/genesis/ch21/v13-21/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/deuteronomy/ch33/v1-2/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/habakkuk/ch3/v3/summary.md`
- `bible/ot/isaiah/ch21/v13-17/summary.md`

## Main Use
- Group the geography-heavy material in one place.
- Separate route-mapping claims from the prophet-like-Moses and Paraclete clusters.
- Preserve the distinction between explicit prophecy texts and support texts.

## Objections / Alternate Readings
- Paran, Seir, Teman, and related locations are debated.
- Some uses in the working corpus are historical-fulfillment claims rather than directly textual ones.
